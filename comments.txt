CALIBRATION:
1. Sky flats (5s) → Master flat per (filter, dither)
   - Normalize each flat before combining
   
2. Science frames (empty fields) → Thermal templates per (filter, dither, exptime)
   - Mask sources, median combine
   - OR: Measure at one exptime, scale linearly

REDUCTION:
1. Thermal correction (if significant)
2. Flat fielding
3. Leveling (median subtraction)
4. Sky pattern (leave-one-out median of N-1 frames)
5. Sky subtraction
6. Alignment (drizzling)
7. Coadd
